My Grimdark Tyranids Lictors. New paint scheme for my Tyranids.

<RecipeBlock>

Recipe Steps

1. Zenith prime
2. Army painter grim black speed paint for carapace (and alien base bits)
3. Army Painter Worn Stone 50% thinner medium for all the skin (and alien base bits)
4. Army painter Worn stone 50% thinner medium for carapace edge highlight (and alien base bits)
5. Army Painter Moody Mauve and 33% thinner medium for vents and holes
6. Army Painter Moody Mauve and 66% thinner medium for glaze around joints and holes and joints
7. Army painter strong Tone 50% thinner medium cover the skin (and alien base bits)
8. Jungle base with Absolution Green and Dark Wood
9. Army painter orc skin speed paint for alien plants.
